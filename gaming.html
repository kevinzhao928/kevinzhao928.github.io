Counter Strike: Global Offensive

Counter Strike: Global Offensive is a game that I played for quite awhile and pretty seriously. I was able to achieve the highest rank in CS:GO possible: Global Elite - in the top 0.75% of all CS:GO players. I was hired to play professionally however the Chinese company that owned the team eventually decided to bail on CS:GO and all the players were dropped. During my time on the team we were sponsored with the most desirable and expensive weapon skins such as an AWP Dragon Lore and an AK-47 Fire Serpent and well as lengendary tier items like Factory New Ruby M9 Bayonets. After being dropped I sold my Steam account along with the skins to a wealthy skin collector and stopped playing CS:GO ever since.
